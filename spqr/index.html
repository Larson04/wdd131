Get ready to experience the magic of adventure as you learn the basics of survival in the camp.

Welcome to Camp Jupiter! We are so excited for you to join us! Here we have a wide range of activities to choose from.

We serve roughly 200 campers each year. Campers are divided into five cohorts with roughly 40 campers per cohort. This will be the group that you live, train, and go through our other activities with.

Our camp likes to focus on self defense and survival, so we like to start our new members with some basic training. The first combat lessons you will receive will be with a pugio, or a small dagger, and shield. As you progress through training you can move up to cavalry and galdiator techniques as well as archery. Our more advanced members will be able to move up to the siege weapons like the scorpion ballista.

Every two weeks we bring all of our campers together and play some war games!
